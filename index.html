About

System of Beauty is a design study that examines the makeup routine as a framework for understanding beauty culture and its design histories. Presented through a physical accordion publication and a corresponding digital timeline, the project uses the familiar sequence of cosmetic application to organize research on the cultural and societal values embedded in each product. By tracing how everyday tools of self-presentation have evolved, both materially and ideologically, the work reframes a private ritual as a collective reflection on how design perpetuates ideals of beauty, identity, and transformation.
